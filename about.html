ABOUT US

Hats 4 Cats is a charity that just for $4 a day you can give a hat to a cat. For every donation given, a prisoner in a correctional facility that participates in an animal rehabilitation program involving cats makes a hat for a cat. Animal rehabilitation programs are so successful that prisoners are so excited to find more ways to bond with their animals and show and introduce them to love and trust as most sheltered cats are common to come from backgrounds of abuse and mistreatment. These programs teach the cats socialization meanwhile Hats 4 Cats focuses on helping prisoners bond with their feline fur babies and of course make the cats more fashionable. Animal rehabilitation programs have come to be met with not so much a positive stigma from many meanwhile the program has come to prove very successful, hats for cats means to help prisoners show empathy for our feline friends and help prove how successful the programs are.
